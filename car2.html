description

Expected to be the lowest-emitting and most powerful Sorento, the PHEV model features a turbo-hybrid powertrain combining a water-cooled 13.8 kWh battery pack and 66.9 kW (90 HP / 91 PS) electric motor with a 1.6-liter T-GDi engine and six-speed automatic transmission. On its own, the turbocharged four-cylinder gasoline unit delivers 178 HP (180 PS) and 265 Nm (195 lb-ft) of torque. Factor in the electric motor with its 90 HP (91 PS) and 304 Nm (224 lb-ft) of torque, and the Sorento Plug-in Hybrid offers a combined output of 265 PS (262 HP) and 350 Nm (258 lb-ft) of torque.
